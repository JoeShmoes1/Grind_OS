Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent support issues, premium users receive priority assistance.
Can I request a feature?
Absolutely! We love hearing from our users about features they'd like to see. Use the contact form and select "Product Feedback" as the subject.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for our premium plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team within 14 days of purchase.
How can I report a bug?
If you encounter a bug, please contact our support team with details about the issue, including steps to reproduce it and screenshots if possible.
